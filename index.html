Welcome

You Are Welcome Here

El Shaddai Church is a vibrant, multicultural Christian community based in Aberdeen, Scotland. We are a family of believers committed to worshipping God, growing in His Word, and reaching our community with the love of Jesus Christ.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church family to call home, our doors — and hearts — are open wide for you.

"How good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity."
– Psalm 133:1
