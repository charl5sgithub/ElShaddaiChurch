Welcome to El Shaddai Church

We are a loving Christian community committed to worship, prayer, fellowship, and serving the community. Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a church family, we welcome you with open hearts.

Founded with a vision to be a "Home away from Home," we celebrate diversity and unity in the Body of Christ. Join us as we encounter God's presence and grow together in His grace.
