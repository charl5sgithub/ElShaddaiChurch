Welcoming Spaces

Facility Usage Information

El Shaddai Church offers welcoming spaces for meetings, prayer groups, community gatherings, and ministry events. Our rooms are designed to be versatile, providing a peaceful environment for various activities.

Our facilities are available for church members and approved community use. We take pride in maintaining a clean, modern, and accessible environment for all our visitors.

  • Modern facilities with flexible layouts
  • Suitable for meetings and fellowship
  • Booking approval required for all requests
  • Subject to church calendar availability
