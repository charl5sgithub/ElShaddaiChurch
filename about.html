A Word from the Pastor

Rev. Angeline S. Henry

Senior Pastor, El Shaddai Church Aberdeen

Greetings in the risen name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ!

I thank God for giving us the opportunity to come together in person and worship the living God together. There is something uniquely powerful about gathering as one body, lifting our voices and hearts in praise and adoration.

"How good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity."
– Psalm 133:1

Thank you for visiting our site. I pray that this will be a life-transforming experience for you as it has been for many who have walked through our doors. Whether you are searching for God, or simply looking for a church family to belong to, you are in the right place.

Our church is built on the Word of God and powered by the Holy Spirit. We believe every person is valuable, loved by God, and created with a unique purpose. Come and discover yours with us.

God Bless,
Rev. Angeline S. Henry

What We Believe

Declaration of Faith

The core beliefs that anchor and guide everything we do.

✝️

The Holy Trinity

We believe in the Holy Trinity: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit — three distinct persons, one God, co-equal and co-eternal.

Matthew 28:19 · 2 Corinthians 13:14

👑

Jesus Christ – Lord and Saviour

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God who became flesh, died for our sins, was buried, and rose again on the third day. He is the only way to salvation and the head of the Church.

John 3:16 · Romans 10:9 · 1 Corinthians 15:3-4

📖

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — the ultimate authority for all matters of faith and conduct. It is living, active, and transformative.

2 Timothy 3:16-17 · Hebrews 4:12

🔥

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the ministry and gifts of the Holy Spirit. Every believer can be filled with the Spirit, empowered for service, and led by Him in daily life.

Acts 2:38 · 1 Corinthians 12:4-11 · Galatians 5:22-23

💒

The Universal Church

We believe in the universal church as the body of Christ, made up of all believers across denominational lines who have committed their lives to Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour.

1 Corinthians 12:27 · Ephesians 1:22-23

🌅

The Return of Jesus Christ

We believe in the physical, glorious return of Jesus Christ to judge the living and the dead, and to establish His eternal kingdom. This blessed hope motivates holy living and urgent mission.

1 Thessalonians 4:16-17 · Revelation 22:12
