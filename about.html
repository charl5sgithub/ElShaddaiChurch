Our Heart & Mission

Discover the vision that drives us and the family that makes El Shaddai Church a home away from home.

Our Servant Leaders

Rev. Angeline S. Henry

Greetings in the precious name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ! I am honoured to welcome you to our community.

El Shaddai Church Aberdeen was founded on a vision to see lives transformed by the power of God's Word and the warmth of His love. We believe that everyone who walks through our doors is there by divine appointment.

"Our mission is simple: to know God more deeply and to make Him known more widely in our beautiful city of Aberdeen."

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a place to call home, there is a seat at the table for you here.

Declaration of Faith

📖

The Holy Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — our ultimate authority for faith and conduct.

✝️

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

🕊️

Salvation

We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ and His sacrifice.
