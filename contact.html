First Visit

Plan Your Visit

Here's everything you need to know before coming for the first time.

👚

What to Wear

Come as you are! We are a casual, welcoming church. Smart casual to formal — all are welcome here.

🏛️

When to Arrive

We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes early. Our team will greet you and help you get settled comfortably.

🧒

Children's Programme

We have a safe, fun children's programme running during the Sunday service for kids aged 3–12.

🚗

Parking

Street parking is available nearby. Public transport links also serve the area conveniently.

🤝

First-Time Guests

Look out for our welcoming team at the entrance. We'll make sure you feel right at home from your first moment.

☕

After Service

Enjoy tea, coffee, and great conversation after every Sunday service. It's a wonderful time to connect and make new friends.

🙏

We Can't Wait to Welcome You!

Have questions before your first visit? Feel free to reach out — we'd love to help you feel prepared and excited about joining us.
