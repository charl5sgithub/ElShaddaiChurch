Our Ministries

Find your place to serve, grow, and connect with fellow believers.

At El Shaddai Church, we believe every member has God-given gifts to contribute to the body of Christ. Our ministries provide meaningful opportunities to use those gifts, build community, and impact lives for the Kingdom of God.
